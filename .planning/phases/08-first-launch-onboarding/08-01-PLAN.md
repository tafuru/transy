<objective>
Automatically show Accessibility permission guidance when Transy launches without AX permission.

Purpose: New users currently must discover the guidance window by triggering the hotkey or opening the menu bar icon. This plan makes the guidance proactive — it appears immediately on launch if AX is not granted, so users are never stuck wondering why the app isn't working.

Output: Modified AppDelegate with proactive AX check, enhanced GuidanceView with "why" explanation text and safe URL handling.
</objective>
